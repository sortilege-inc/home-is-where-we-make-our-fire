**Star-Pendant of the Dúnedain (Heirloom)**

- **Description:** A silver pendant bearing the star of the Dúnedain, an old keepsake from her guardians Brandor and Orlec. Its edges are worn smooth from years of wear.

- **Effects:**

- **Symbol of Legacy:** **+1d to Courtesy and Insight Tests** when dealing with other Dúnedain, Elves, or allies of Númenor’s legacy.

- **Defender’s Resolve:** Spending a point of Hope while wearing the pendant grants Celenneth **1 Temporary Endurance** until the end of the scene.

- **Narrative Tie:** The pendant is deeply personal, representing her bond with her guardians and her past. It was the first relic she received and serves as her constant link to her origins.

- Star-Laced Cloak of the North (Marvelous item - armor upgrade)

- **Description:** A travel cloak woven from thick Númenórean wool and laced with faint star-like silver threads that shimmer under the moonlight.

- **Effects:**

- **Hidden in the Wilds:** **+1d to Stealth and Travel rolls** when navigating forests, mountains, or desolate terrain.

- **Ward Against Frost:** The cloak’s starry pattern **reduces Fatigue from cold weather by 1 point.**

- **Narrative Tie:** This cloak was found wrapped around ancient scrolls in the cache, as though protecting them from time’s decay. It was likely worn by Númenórean envoys tasked with delivering critical lore.
